About Focus Timer

Focus Timer is a simple yet powerful productivity tool designed to help you stay focused, organized, and mentally balanced. Based on the proven Pomodoro Technique, it encourages short, timed work sessions followed by breaks, maximizing your concentration and reducing burnout.

With integrated features like a to-do list for task management and a moodbar for setting the tone of your workspace, Focus Timer is more than just a timer — it's your personal productivity companion. Whether you're studying, working remotely, or just trying to manage your time better, our tool helps you stay on track.

Built for simplicity and effectiveness, Focus Timer is fully browser-based, with no account needed to start. Your tasks and preferences are saved locally for privacy and ease of use.

Stay focused. Stay productive. Stay balanced — with Focus Timer.
