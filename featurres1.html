App Features

A brief look at the core functionalities of our focus timer application.

Pomodoro Timer

Utilize the Pomodoro Technique to work in focused intervals. Customize session lengths for both work and short breaks to maximize productivity.

Integrated To-Do List

Keep track of your tasks directly within the app. Add, edit, and check off items as you complete them, helping you stay organized and on top of your work.

Daily Mood Tracker

Record your mood at the beginning or end of each focus session. This feature helps you gain insight into how your emotional state affects your productivity over time.
